Word List ?The word list is divided into two parts. The 'Active Words' and 'Word Que'. 'Active Words' are words that are replaced whenever you visit a site. The 'Word Que' are words that are next up to be added to the 'Active Words' list. The default words uses Simplified Chinese but you can use any language you like.

Excluded Sites?Sites included here are excluded from word replacement. Partial matches are also excluded. For example if someone entered 'red'. Then 'reddit.com', 'alfred.com', and 'redsite.com' would be excluded from word replacement. Similarly if someone entered 'reddit.com' then all reddit.com subdomains and domains would be excluded (for instance reddit.com/r/music and http://www.reddit.com/r/pics would be excluded). 'google.com/search' is disabled by default since this extension seems not to play nicely.
